Early Life

She was born in McComb, Mississippi on February 11, 1979. Then in 1983, her family moved to Los Angeles, California.

Musical Career

In 1993, Brandy signed with Atlantic Records at the age of 14. A year later, she released her self-titled debut album and it sold over six million copies worldwide. As of 2022, Brandy has released 7 studio albums.

Acting Career

  • 1993 - Brandy starred in "Thea" and earned a Young Artists Award nomination.
  • 1996 - She then went on to star in the sitcom "Moesha" and the show ran for 6 seasons.
  • 1997 - Brandy was the first Black actress to play the role of Cinderella on-screen.
  • 2012 - She played the character of Chardonay Pitts in the series "The Game".
  • 2018 - Brandy also starred in the hit television show "Star" as Cassie.
