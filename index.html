Brandy Rayana Norwood

Thee Vocal Bible

Early Life

She was born in McComb, Mississippi on February 11, 1979. Then in 1983, her family moved to Los Angeles, California.

Musical Career

In 1993, Brandy signed with Atlantic Records at the age of 14. A year later, she released her self-titled debut album and it sold over six million copies worldwide.

Top Selling Records

  • "Have You Ever"
  • "The Boy Is Mine"

Acting Career

  • 1993- Brandy starred in "Thea" and earned a Young Artits Award nomination.
  • 1996- Brandy starred in the sitcom Moesha and the show ran for 6 seasons.
